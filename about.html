ABOUT FTH

Faith Industries (FTH) is a clothing brand based in Bandung, Indonesia, founded in November 2019 by Luthfi Halimawan and Rizal Malik. Deeply rooted in the worlds of anime, music, gaming, and Japanese pop culture, FTH was built to bridge fandom with fashion through bold storytelling and streetwear aesthetics. Since our inception, we've proudly collaborated with numerous official anime licenses and global icons, including Jujutsu Kaisen, Chainsaw Man, Dr. Stone, Evangelion, PAC-MAN, and more. Every collection is crafted with passion, experimentation, and cultural relevance, turning each piece into more than just clothing, but a statement of identity and connection.
